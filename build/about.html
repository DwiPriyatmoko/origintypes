Prologue

The Pursuit of typing experience

I believe in this modern world almost everyone spent their time in front of the desktop. One of the desktop components is a keyboard which is one tool for input anything we want to do on it. As time goes by, the keyboard is evolved to not just a typing tool, but far from that, it's become a tool that turns into a hobby especially a custom mechanical keyboard.

A custom mechanical keyboard is changing the experience of typing into something heavenly and giving pleasure to every force when type on it. I want to share this pleasure with everyone because they deserve the best feel on their board.
