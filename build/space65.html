Space65 Cybervoyager

CyberVoyager is a keyboard made by Graystudio based on the original Space65, it is redesigned by AirPotter, Oldcat, and Demos to make it a more awesome board.

List of mod

  • Holy Panda lube with Krytox 205g0 & GPL 105

  • Durock V2 Stabilizer lube with Krytox 205g0

  • Desky Switch Film

  • Caps lock LED Indicator

Adorable 65% Keyboard

Space65 Cybervoyager mechanical keyboard has 65% layout with top mounting system, this board has a unique shape from others custom mechanical keyboard.

This board has Carbon Fiber plate as default and aluminum and FR4 as an option. PCB of this board is solder version and doesn't have Hotswap option.
